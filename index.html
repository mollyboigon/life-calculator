How are you spending your life?

By Molly Boigon

The average person lives for 79 years, or 28,835 days.

Within those 79 years, 33 of them are spent in bed, either sleeping or trying to sleep.

More than 13 of those years are spent at work.

The average person spends more than eight years watching TV.

Nearly five years are spent eating.
